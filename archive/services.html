FAQ

Common questions

Do you work with a single cloud or all four?
We work across all four: AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI. Most of our clients are on AWS or Azure, but we have production experience on all platforms. If you're multi-cloud, we can span them — that's genuinely rare in the market.
How does the 50% upfront payment work?
For project work, we invoice 50% at contract signing and 50% upon delivery and acceptance. Retainers are invoiced monthly in advance. Productized packages are paid in full before we start — they're pre-scoped so there's no ambiguity.
What if the scope changes mid-project?
Any scope change requires a signed change request before we do any additional work. We define this clearly in our SOW upfront. It protects both sides — you get a clear price before we proceed, and we don't do unpaid work.
Who actually does the work — are there junior staff involved?
No. Every engagement is led and delivered by one of our senior engineers. We are a small, specialist team by design. You will never be handed off to someone more junior after signing — that's one of the reasons we exist.
Do we need to give you access to our cloud accounts?
For most engagements, yes. We follow least-privilege access principles and only request the permissions needed for the specific task. We use role-based access (IAM roles, service principals) — never root/admin credentials. We sign an NDA before any access is granted.
