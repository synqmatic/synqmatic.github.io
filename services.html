// 03 — FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with a single cloud or all four?
All four: AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI. Most of our clients are on AWS or Azure, but we have hands-on production experience across all platforms. Multi-cloud spanning is genuinely rare — it's one of the reasons we exist.
How does the 50% upfront payment work?
We invoice 50% at contract signing and 50% upon delivery and client acceptance. The scope is fixed in the SOW before we start, so there's no ambiguity on either side.
What happens if scope changes mid-project?
Any change to deliverables requires a signed change request before we do any additional work. We define this clearly in the SOW upfront. It protects both sides — you get a clear price before we proceed, and we don't do unpaid work.
Do you need access to our cloud accounts?
For most engagements, yes. We follow least-privilege access principles and only request the permissions needed for the specific task. We use role-based access — IAM roles, service principals, workload identity — never root or admin credentials. We sign an NDA before any access is granted.
